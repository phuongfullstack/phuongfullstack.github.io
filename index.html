About Me

My Journey & Approach

From my beginnings at Duy Tan University, I've cultivated a passion for building software that not only functions flawlessly but also delivers tangible value. Over the past 6+ years, I've had the privilege of contributing to diverse projects at companies like FPT Software, Axon Active, and Propel Ventures, tackling challenges in finance, healthcare, and gaming.

My core strength lies in full-stack development, particularly within the .NET ecosystem and cloud platforms like Azure. I believe in writing clean, maintainable code following principles like SOLID and Clean Architecture, and I thrive on optimizing applications for peak performance and robust security. Whether it's designing scalable APIs, crafting intuitive user interfaces, or streamlining DevOps processes, I'm driven by the challenge of finding elegant solutions to complex problems.
